    • Order & Delivery

    Shipping is available in three options
    1) Targeted delivery via postal services like UPS or FedEx. This kind of delivery is available for shipments of small items, porcelain portraits, carved figurines, carved vases, flowerpots and other decor weighing less than 150 pounds.
    2) Delivery by airfreight is beneficial for goods from 150 to 1000 pounds. Airfreight allow to ship products to your nearest international airport only.
    3) Sea freight is most beneficial for cargo over 1000 pounds. This is the slowest type of transportation, but during delivering memorial complexes, the cost of transportation could be in ten times lower then airfreight. In case bulk delivering about 100 monuments, delivery of one unit in a 20-ton container will cost about 40-50$ per one unit.
    The most important thing in delivery is the correct and reliable packaging. We ship all products in strong plywood boxes on pallets; product is always tightly compressed with foam inside the box. Such packaging allows us to give a 100% guarantee for delivery of goods without damage.
